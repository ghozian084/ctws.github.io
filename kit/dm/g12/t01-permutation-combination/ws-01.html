🏃 Motoric Action 4: Group Hugs (Combinations)

Action: Call 4 students to the front. We need to select a "Team" of 2.
If Andi and Budi are selected, have them form a circle/hug. Is "Andi & Budi" different from "Budi & Andi" in a circle? No! Order DOES NOT matter.

🧠 Mindful Math Connection

Since order doesn't matter, we have fewer options than a Permutation. We use Combinations ($^nC_r$). The formula divides out the extra arrangements: $^nC_r = \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}$
